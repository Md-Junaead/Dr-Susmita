About the Doctor

A Life Devoted to
Women's Health & Hope

Dr. Susmita Sarker is a distinguished consultant in Gynecological Oncology and Gynaecology & Obstetrics with over 15 years of clinical expertise. She holds dual FCPS degrees and has dedicated her career to the diagnosis, treatment, and care of women facing complex gynecological conditions and cancers.

Practicing at Green Life Hospital Ltd., Dhaka, and the National Institute of Cancer Research & Hospital (NICRH), she combines cutting-edge medical knowledge with a deeply compassionate approach — ensuring every patient feels heard, supported, and hopeful.
